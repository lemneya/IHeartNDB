# Skill — Weekly Campaign Review

## Purpose
Summarize the week for the founder and propose next week's direction.

## Inputs
- The week's approved messages (`memory/approved_messages.md`)
- Engagement / sentiment notes
- Active gate status

## Process
1. Review what was approved and published.
2. Note what worked (emotion, action uptake) and what stalled.
3. Keep it honest — report misses plainly, no inflated numbers.
4. Propose a focused plan for next week (max 3 priorities).

## Output structure
```
# I❤️NDB Weekly Review — [week of date]

## What we did
- [approved + published items]

## What worked
- [observation]

## What stalled / missed
- [honest note]

## Metrics (verified only)
- [metric]: [value or "not measured"]

## Next week — top 3
1. [priority]
2. [priority]
3. [priority]

## Needs founder decision
- [ ] [item]
```

## Quality checklist
- [ ] Honest about misses
- [ ] No invented metrics
- [ ] Max 3 priorities
- [ ] Clear founder decision list
