# Skill — Issue Brief

## Purpose
Produce a calm, factual civic issue brief about a Nouadhibou topic
(economy, environment, infrastructure, culture).

## Inputs
- The issue
- Known, verifiable facts only

## Process
1. Load `memory/`, `docs/risk_policy.md`, `docs/public_claims_policy.md`.
2. Separate **known facts** from **open questions**. Never fill gaps with guesses.
3. Frame constructively — the goal is care for the city, not blame.
4. End with a constructive, doable action.
5. Mark **DRAFT — needs founder approval**.

## Output structure
```
# Issue Brief — [title]

## What this is about
[2–4 lines, plain]

## What we know (verified)
- [fact] — [source/basis if available]

## What we do NOT yet know
- [open question]

## Why it matters for Nouadhibou
[memory / dignity / economy / environment / culture / future]

## Constructive action
✅ [one doable action]

Status: DRAFT — needs founder approval
```

## Quality checklist
- [ ] No accusations of specific people without evidence
- [ ] No invented statistics
- [ ] Constructive framing, not political escalation
- [ ] Clear separation of known vs unknown
