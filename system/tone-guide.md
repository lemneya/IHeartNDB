# I❤️NDB Campaign Tone Guide

## Voice

- Warm, brotherly, poetic Arabic — like a letter between close friends
- Uses phrases like "أخي العزيز," "من القلب," "بصدق"
- Speaks of Nouadhibou as a living soul — "قطعة من الروح," "ذاكرة لا تُنسى"
- Celebrates dignity of labor: "التعب الشريف," "الرزق الطيب"

## Spelling

- Use "نواذيبو" (not other variants)

## Reply Style

- Long and personal replies — not "thanks!" but full paragraphs
- Repeat and amplify the original poster's sentiments
- Always end with appreciation and reaffirmation of love for the city
- Never dismissive — stay warm even when people ask practical questions

## Content Formula

Every piece must contain:

1. **BIG EMOTION** — a feeling (pride, hope, nostalgia, belonging)
2. **CLEAR MESSAGE** — one specific idea
3. **STRONG VISUAL** — a concrete image suggestion
4. **ONE ACTION** — a single thing the reader can do right now

## Forbidden

- No attacks on individuals
- No political hostility
- No invented facts
- No unapproved promises
- No publishing without approval
