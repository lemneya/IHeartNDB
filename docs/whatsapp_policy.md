# WhatsApp Policy

WhatsApp is intimate and easily abused. I❤️NDB treats it with extra care.

## Principles

1. **Consent-aware** — messages are written to be shared by willing people,
   not blasted to harvested contact lists.
2. **Respectful** — Hassaniya warmth, polite greeting, never pushy.
3. **Short** — forwardable in one screen; no long threads.
4. **One action** — exactly one simple, optional, low-cost action.
5. **Easy to ignore** — never guilt, pressure, or shame for not forwarding.
6. **No spam** — no repetitive blasts, no flooding groups, no chain-letter
   pressure ("send to 10 people or…").

## Allowed
- A short, warm message a supporter chooses to forward to people who would
  welcome it.
- Event/info notices that are relevant and timely.

## Not allowed
- Mass unsolicited sending to strangers.
- Using lists collected without people's awareness.
- Political pressure, religious pressure, or fear framing.
- Stating unverified claims or numbers.

## Required structure
```
[Warm greeting — سلام عليكم ❤️]
[Short emotional hook — Hassaniya warmth, 1–2 lines]
[Clear message — 1 line]
✅ المطلوب: [one optional action]
#I❤️NDB
```

All WhatsApp drafts also pass `docs/risk_policy.md` and need founder approval.
