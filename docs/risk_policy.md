# Risk Policy

Binding for all I❤️NDB output. If a draft conflicts with this policy, the
policy wins.

## Prohibited

1. **Political escalation** — no content that inflames political conflict or
   takes partisan sides.
2. **Accusing specific people without evidence** — never name or target an
   individual with blame unless there is verified, citable evidence, and even
   then only with founder approval.
3. **Fake statistics** — no invented, estimated-as-fact, or unverifiable
   numbers. "Remembered role" language is allowed; fabricated figures are not.
4. **Religious or political manipulation** — do not use faith or political
   identity to pressure, divide, or mobilize against others.
5. **Spam** — no mass unsolicited messaging, no flooding, no repetitive
   blasting across channels.

## Required

6. **WhatsApp must be respectful and consent-aware** — see
   `docs/whatsapp_policy.md`. No pressure, no harvested lists, easy to ignore.
7. **Public content must be approved before publishing** — founder gate is
   mandatory. Hermes output is DRAFT until logged in
   `memory/approved_messages.md`.

## Tone safeguards

- No insults, ever — toward any person, group, region, or institution.
- Stay respectful even under provocation; de-escalate, don't fight.
- Unify, never divide.

## Risk check (run before any draft is sent to the founder)

- [ ] No political escalation
- [ ] No accusations of specific people without evidence
- [ ] No fake or unverifiable statistics
- [ ] No religious/political manipulation
- [ ] Not spam; consent-aware on WhatsApp
- [ ] Exactly one clear action
- [ ] Marked DRAFT — needs founder approval

If any box fails, the draft is rejected and revised before reaching the founder.
