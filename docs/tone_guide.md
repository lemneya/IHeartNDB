# Tone Guide

The voice of I❤️NDB. Binding for all content.

## Voice

- Warm, brotherly, poetic Arabic — like a letter between close friends.
- Phrases such as «أخي العزيز», «من القلب», «بصدق».
- Speak of Nouadhibou as a living soul: «قطعة من الروح», «ذاكرة لا تُنسى».
- Celebrate the dignity of honest labor: «التعب الشريف», «الرزق الطيب».
- Hassaniya warmth and idiom for WhatsApp / spoken / local content.

## Languages

- **Arabic first** (default). Spelling: **نواذيبو** (no other variant).
- **Hassaniya-aware** for intimate/local channels.
- **French** and **English** as concise summaries when needed.

## The formula (every piece)

1. **Big emotion** — pride, hope, nostalgia, belonging.
2. **Clear message** — one specific idea.
3. **Strong visual** — one concrete image suggestion.
4. **One action** — one thing the reader can do now.

## Reply style

- Long, personal, warm — not "thanks!" but full sentences.
- Repeat and amplify the original poster's sentiment.
- End with appreciation and reaffirmed love for the city.
- Never dismissive; stay warm even to critics.

## Emotional palette

Pride · Nostalgia · Belonging · Hope · Dignity of work · Unity.

## Forbidden in tone

- No attacks, no insults, no political hostility.
- No invented facts or numbers.
- No unapproved promises.
- No publishing without founder approval.
