# Memory — City Identity: Nouadhibou (نواذيبو)

## Name and spelling

- Arabic (authoritative spelling): **نواذيبو**
- French / Latin: **Nouadhibou**
- Short tag: **NDB**
- Always use **نواذيبو** in Arabic content. Do not use other variants.

## Identity in one line

Nouadhibou is **Mauritania's remembered economic capital** — a working,
sea-facing city built by honest labor.

## Pillars of the city's identity

1. **The sea** — the rhythm of the city; fishing as livelihood and heritage.
2. **The port** — the gateway; trade, movement, and connection.
3. **Honest work** — dignity of labor; "الرزق يأتي من العمل الشريف".
4. **Industry and logistics** — fish processing, services, iron-ore logistics link.
5. **Strategic position** — northern coastal crossroads, regional reach.
6. **People and culture** — simple faces, working hands, Hassaniya warmth,
   a small homeland inside the larger homeland.
7. **Environment** — coastline, dunes, marine life worth protecting.

## Emotional anchors (use these feelings)

- Pride — "this city built things."
- Nostalgia — the smell of the sea at dawn, fathers' stories.
- Belonging — "a small homeland inside the homeland."
- Hope — the city still deserves the best.

## Voice notes

- Speak of Nouadhibou as a **living soul**: "قطعة من الروح", "ذاكرة لا تُنسى".
- Celebrate honest labor: "التعب الشريف", "الرزق الطيب".
- Warm, brotherly Arabic; Hassaniya idiom for local/WhatsApp.

Identity content celebrates the city. It never attacks a person, group, region,
or institution, and never asserts numbers that are not verified.
